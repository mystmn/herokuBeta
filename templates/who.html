United Facility Resource LLC. was developed by Omar Hinton in 2000 as a commercial cleaning service and a mid-sized construction company. UFR is now a full service facility maintenance operation servicing central Ohio, surrounding areas and businesses throughout the U.S. As a result of our dedication, hard work, and our commitment to excellence, UFR is primed to become one of central Ohio’s most respected maintenance service providers.

Our approach consists of a lean construction process with the alignment and holistic pursuit of concurrent and continuous improvements. What does that all mean? It means when working with our company we dedicate ourselves to finding you the most economic solutions coupled with the industries most talented and knowledgeable service technicians.

Our project coordinators have over 30 years of industry experience. They are continually trained on the important responsibility of planning, execution and the ability to adapt ensuring that the key issues of cost, time, quality and above all, client satisfaction is realized.

UFR is the future of maintenance outsourcing. We don’t strive to be the largest most well know service provider on the planet. We focus on quality and customer satisfaction. Your return business is what keeps us successful. So we say thanks to all of our clients who enjoy our continued success with us and welcome your establishment to be a part of our team.
